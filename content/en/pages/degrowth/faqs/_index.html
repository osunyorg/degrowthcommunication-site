This page links to two comprehensive Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) resources from two leading organisations in the degrowth movement: Ontrgoei, the Dutch Degrowth Network, and Associazione Per La Decrescita, based in Italy. Together, these groups have written over 40 FAQs meticulously organised into categories that cover a wide spectrum of topics. Each question is not only answered with insightful information but is also supported by sources for deeper exploration. Navigate through the topics to find answers, gain new perspectives, and enrich your knowledge.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: call_to_action title: >- ranks: self: false data: text: >-

The Ontrgoei FAQ page offers a detailed exploration of degrowth, available in both Dutch and English. Covering seven key topics, it provides both concise summaries and in-depth responses to each of the frequently asked questions, ensuring accessibility for readers seeking quick insights or thorough understanding. Each answer is thoughtfully cited, allowing for further investigation into the topics discussed. Engaging and informative, this resource is a valuable starting point for anyone curious about the principles and practices of degrowth.

The Associazione Per La Decrescita's FAQ page provides a comprehensive overview of degrowth through 20 well-crafted questions, all presented in Italian. While readers may need to utilise translation software or browser extensions, each question delves into critical aspects of degrowth, offering valuable insights supported by citations. This resource serves as an excellent guide for Italian-speaking audiences and those interested in global perspectives on degrowth, ensuring a broad understanding of its concepts and applications.
