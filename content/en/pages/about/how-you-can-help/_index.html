Your contributions can take many forms, each vital to enhancing the quality, reach, and impact of our work. Here's how you can make a difference:

  • Engage in Fact-Checking: We aspire to uphold the highest standards of accuracy and reliability. Consider lending your expertise in conducting a thorough review of our content, ensuring that every piece of information stands up to rigorous scrutiny.
  • Share Your Insights: Your feedback is invaluable. You can reach out to us for any reason at all! Help us uncover gaps in our content and identify opportunities for improvement.
  • Spread the Word: Share our site! Every mention helps build momentum for change.
  • Craft Your Content: Use your voice to create content that echoes degrowth ideals. Blogs, art, or discussions can all inspire action.
  • Develop Targeted Guides: Contribute to the creation of communication guides tailored to various demographics, enabling more effective discourse around degrowth principles and practices.
  • Craft Educational Material: Assist in designing presentation slides and educational materials that resonate with different groups, enriching our educational outreach.
  • Expand Our Content: The degrowth conversation is ever-evolving. Help us stay ahead by contributing new findings, correcting misconceptions, and enriching our platform with the latest narratives, facts, and visual aids.
  • Mentor and Collaborate: Share this platform with students and emerging researchers looking to expand their knowledge on degrowth, fostering a community of learning and innovation.
  • Establish Guidelines: Aid in the creation of clear guidelines for content addition and site management, ensuring the platform's longevity and relevance.
  • Promote Digital Accessibility: Know any digital accessibility experts who could review our site pro bono? This will make sure our resources are available to everyone.
  • Translate Content: Broaden our reach by translating our materials into various languages, making degrowth accessible to a global audience.
