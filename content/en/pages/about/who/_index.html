Hi there, I'm JP, a descendant of European settlers in México, now making my home in Paris. I've dedicated myself to using the privilege I hold to pursue a world where people collectively plan the fair distribution of resources to provide well-being for all within ecological limits. My journey has led me to the heart of the degrowth movement, a space where I have felt welcomed and loved. I believe there is a lot of work ahead of us, and I just hope this project can help push us a little bit closer to our goals. 

I am also happy to let you all know that the Communications Circle of the International Degrowth Network - external link (IDN) has agreed to take on ownership and maintenance of this website to ensure its long-term sustainability and democratic management. 

I want to emphasise that the IDN does not currently endorse any of the content on the site, as it is still under internal review. 

Creating, designing, and curating the content for this website has been a labour of love, guided by the belief that empathy, humility and accessible knowledge are crucial for our struggle. This project is a testament to the collaborative spirit of the degrowth movement, involving dozens of individuals and organisations who share a vision for a sustainable and equitable future. Their invaluable, beautiful and selfless contributions are recognised and celebrated below.
