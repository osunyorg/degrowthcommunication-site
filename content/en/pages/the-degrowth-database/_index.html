In this digital library, you can find

  • Details of over 300 groups worldwide doing incredible work in the degrowth space.
  • 1500 academic papers organized into more than 40 categories.
  • A tool (external link) to filter these papers by topic, language, publication type and more.
  • 450 policy proposals that advance degrowth
  • Over 70 pieces of curated media content (documentaries, blogs, and playlists)
  • 550 books discussing degrowth in nearly 30 languages.
  • 100+ news articles from some of the world's most well-known news publishers.
  • ~50 educational programmes listed, from master's degrees to workshops.
  • Various communication channels of public degrowth communities.
  • Details on over 30 degrowth conferences and 4 international assemblies

The database is also helpful to find resources that aren't in English. It's got content in over 30 different languages making degrowth accessible to millions!

This database is constantly growing and improving with contributions from people like you. Whether you’re here to explore, find ways to get involved, or add your own discoveries to the mix, we’re glad you stopped by. Dive in, see what you find, and maybe get inspired to join the conversation.
