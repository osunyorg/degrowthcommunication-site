What is Transformative Learning?

Transformative Learning (TL) describes how people change their understanding of the world around them. People undergo a profound shift in their perspective, beliefs, or worldviews. Typically, this process involves three dimensions: psychological changes in understanding of the self, convictional revision of belief systems, and behavioral changes in lifestyle.
