Post Growth guide

The Post Growth Guide (PGG) offers over 300 resources to empower entrepreneurs and organisations to build and run businesses within the ecological limits of our planet. Join their community of over 10,000 members and start a journey towards sustainable business practices that respect our earth's boundaries.

Visit the Post Growth Guide
https://www.postgrowthguide.com/

Regrowth Marketing for Impact Businesses

Regrowth marketing combines proven principles of growth hacking with a commitment to sustainable, meaningful growth. It’s about helping businesses like yours thrive within planetary boundaries, not chasing growth for its own sake.


Founded in 2020 by Jennifer Wilkins, a specialist in degrowth with a corporate management background, Heliocene is a research and consulting practice focused on business sector transformation towards a post growth economic paradigm.Get in touch at heliocene.nz[at]gmail.com

Business for Sufficiency Database

The Business for Sufficiency database is a tool that showcases companies' efforts to promote sustainable consumption practices. By providing real-world examples, it aims to inspire companies to consider sufficiency as part of their business models.The database was developed through a review of publicly-available data of 150 companies (and growing) that support sufficient consumption across a range of sectors. For each firm, you can find details on the company, a summary of their business model and the strategies used to promote sufficiency, as well as info on the key business model elements (value proposition, creation and delivery, capture).Check it out here.
