Welcome to our dedicated space for exploring the transformative world of degrowth through our curated selection of magazines. Here, you’ll find insightful publications that delve deep into the principles of degrowth, featuring thought-provoking articles, in-depth analyses, and inspiring stories from around the globe. Whether you’re a long-time advocate or new to the concept, our collection of degrowth magazines serves as a vital resource to understand and engage with this crucial movement towards a more equitable and sustainable future.

body: >-


15/15\15 Magazine is a pivotal voice for the degrowth movement in Spain and Portugal, urging a shift toward sustainable living within ecological limits. It uniquely involves its subscribers in shaping its direction, ensuring it remains a grassroots, community-driven publication. Covering topics from radical ecology to peak oil, the magazine fosters a critical dialogue on constructing a sustainable future.

Check them out here!
https://www.15-15-15.org/webzine/en/


Journal de la Décroissance is a critical and engaging publication that promotes the principles of degrowth by challenging the conventional narratives of economic growth and consumerism. Known for its satirical critiques, vibrant illustrations and advocacy for a society that values well-being over wealth, this French journal blends academic insights with practical activism, encouraging a shift toward sustainable and equitable lifestyles.

Check them out here!
http://www.ladecroissance.net/
